ABOUT

Hello there, my name is Peter Abuczki-Muhel. I am a graphic designer based in the wonderful city of Aberdeen, Scotland. When I’m not knee- deep in a project you can find me taking a walk on the shore with my lovely wife or playing some tabletop roleplaying game with some fellow nerds.

I’m passionate about creating well thought out designs with a touch of simplicity and awesomeness, because ‘design’ shouldn’t be the equivalent of ‘overwhelming’.
